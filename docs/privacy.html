Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 Aug 2025

This policy explains how Crazy Tower Rise (“the App”) handles data.

What we collect

  • No account data: We don’t create in‑app accounts or collect names/emails.
  • Game progress: Scores and settings are stored on your device.
  • Game Center (optional): If you sign in, Apple provides a player identifier so leaderboards/achievements work. Apple’s privacy policy applies.
  • Purchases: In‑app purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to validate receipts; it may process purchase receipts and an app‑scoped user ID. See RevenueCat’s policy.
  • Rewarded ads (optional): If you choose to watch an ad for a reward, our ad partner may process device/ad identifiers per their policy.

How we use data

  • Provide gameplay features (scores, daily challenges, leaderboards).
  • Validate purchases and deliver entitlements (e.g., premium).
  • Show optional rewarded ads when requested.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers strictly to operate the App:

  • Apple Game Center (leaderboards/achievements)
  • RevenueCat (purchase validation/entitlements)
  • Ad network for rewarded ads (e.g., Google AdMob) — only if you watch an ad

Retention

Purchase records are retained as required for support and compliance. Gameplay data stays on your device unless synced by Apple services.

Children

The App is intended for a general audience. If you are under the age required by your region, use under a guardian’s guidance.

Your choices

  • Game Center can be disabled in iOS Settings.
  • You can avoid rewarded ads by not opting in.
  • Delete the App to remove local data from your device.
